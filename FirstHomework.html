2. How are resources (html pages, images, etc) addressed in the web?

They are linked with URLS (Uniform Resource Locaters)

3. What are the general components of a url?

http://www.something.at/index.html, which indicates a protocol (http), a hostname (www.something.at), and a file name (index.html).
